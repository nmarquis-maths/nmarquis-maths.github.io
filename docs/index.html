I am currently a post-doc in the working group of Eugen Hellmann at Universität Münster. I completed my PhD at Sorbonne Université under the supervision of Pierre Colmez and Antoine Ducros. My thesis studied multivariable \((\varphi,\Gamma)\)-modules, especially their Lubin-Tate and plectic versions, which now try to apply to questions related to the local \(p\)-adic Langlands correspondance for \(\mathrm{GL}_{2} (K) \).

In my research, I'm mainly interested in \(p\)-adic Hodge theory, the \(p\)-adic Langlands program and the cohomology of \(p\)-adic rigid analytic spaces.
