I am currently a post-doc in the working group of Eugen Hellmann at the University of Münster. I completed my PhD at the Sorbonne University under the supervision of Pierre Colmez and Antoine Ducros, in the Institut de mathématiques Jussieu-Paris rive gauche (IMJ-PRG). My thesis studied multivariable \((\varphi,\Gamma)\)-modules, especially their Lubin-Tate and plectic versions, which I expect to be useful in the search for a local \(p\)-adic Langlands correspondance for \(\mathrm{GL}_2 (K)\).
